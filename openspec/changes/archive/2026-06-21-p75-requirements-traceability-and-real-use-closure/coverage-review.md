## Residual Risk Notes

- P75 executed the traceability audit and runtime knowledge-context fix, but it did not complete every original requirement as `real_pass`.
- Current release conclusion is `release_ready_scoped_with_traceability_gaps`, not `release_ready_full_requirements_traceable`.
- The highest-priority remaining gaps are live public-provider repeatability beyond accepted-local request construction, arbitrary-symbol coverage, full field-level fund/index/benchmark joins, missing funds-flow/margin/constituent-financial/media-heat propagation, full live-provider branch depth for SOPs, and full action-matrix breadth where rows depend on live-provider/arbitrary-symbol coverage.
- The expanded G9 scan found boundary-term matches only in prohibition/boundary, test, historical, captured acceptance, sanitizer, gatekeeper, seed prohibited-action, or non-data-frequency-label contexts; no forbidden runtime affordance was found.
